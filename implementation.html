Decentrafly Implementation Timeline

From site selection to go-live in ~4–6 weeks

Phase 1 Review Locations
Who: Sales & Customer
What: AE presents recommended mounting spots for ESPARGOS sensors; customer reviews Deployment Plan (≥5 locations).
Notes: We pre-run RF & line-of-sight models. Start HOA/municipal notice if needed.
Phase 2 Finalize Locations
Sign Service & Data-Sharing Agreement, kick off permits, arrange electrician if using AC power. Rooftop installs receive stamped drawings.
Phase 3 Site Survey & Flagging
Field engineer validates solar exposure, RF noise floor, structural integrity, and FirstNet/LTE signal; flags install spots. Alternate spots are proposed if anything fails.
Phase 4 811 Utility Locate
Decentrafly files 811 tickets for every ground-mounted pole/trench (10 ft radius). Turnaround varies by state and is outside our control.
Phase 5 Schedule Installation
PM books crew window and ships any specialty hardware (receiver masts, “Just-a-Jetson” edge servers, FirstNet SIMs) to arrive 2–3 days ahead.
Phase 6 Install & Commission
Crew mounts hardware, aligns antenna array, activates backhaul, and runs acceptance tests (GNSS lock, mesh heartbeat, Remote ID capture, AWS GovCloud ingest). Baseline RF spectrum screenshot is recorded.
Phase 7 Training & Go-Live
Customer Success Manager (virtual) delivers portal training, alert setup, CJIS onboarding, and referral-program briefing. Teams are normally trained in under two hours.
Phase 8 Ongoing Support
24 × 7 NOC monitoring, OTA firmware updates, quarterly coverage audits. Additional ESPARGOS units dispatched if gaps appear.
