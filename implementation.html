Project Roll-Out Process

From site selection to go-live in about 4–6 weeks

Step 1 Review Locations
Who: Decentrafly & Customer
What: We present recommended camera or sensor locations; you review and approve a deployment plan (minimum five sites to kick off).
Step 2 Finalize Agreements
Sign the service agreement, begin any local permit applications, and confirm power or mounting needs with your facilities team.
Step 3 Site Walk & Marking
A field specialist visits each site to confirm visibility, safety, and connectivity. Once validated, small white flags mark every approved spot.
Step 4 Utility Clearance
We coordinate “Call 811” so local crews can mark underground utilities within a 10-foot radius of each flagged site.
Step 5 Schedule Installation
When flags are cleared, we lock an install date and ship any site-specific materials to arrive a few days ahead of the crew.
Step 6 Install & Check
Our team mounts the equipment, activates connectivity, and verifies live data flow. You receive a simple “all-green” report the same day.
Step 7 Training & Launch
A Customer Success Manager walks your team through the web portal, alerts, and best-practice playbooks. Most teams are fully trained in under two hours.
Step 8 Ongoing Support
Decentrafly provides 24 × 7 system monitoring, remote updates, and quarterly coverage reviews. If a gap appears, we resolve it at no extra cost.
